    126 Projects and $0 Dollars later: What I Learned About Value Creation¶

    Introduction¶

    Can you tell us about your journey on GitHub and how you ended up with 126 projects?¶

    • Share your background and how you started using GitHub.

    What was your initial motivation for creating so many projects?¶

    • Discuss your early goals and what drove you to build so many repositories.

    The Early Days¶

    What were some of your first projects, and what did you hope to achieve with them?¶

    • Talk about your initial projects and your expectations.

    Did you have any early successes or moments of excitement?¶

    • Share any early wins or moments that fueled your passion.

    Were there any signs early on that these projects might not lead to financial success?¶

    • Reflect on any early indicators that the projects weren’t financially viable.

    The Turning Point¶

    When did you first realize that completing projects wasn’t leading to financial success?¶

    • Describe the moment or period when you had this realization.

    What specific project or experience made you rethink your approach?¶

    • Provide a detailed story of a project that highlighted the need for a change in strategy.

    How did this realization impact your mindset and approach to project creation?¶

    • Explain how your thinking and approach began to shift.

    Lessons Learned¶

    What are the key lessons you learned about value creation versus sheer output?¶

    • Summarize the main insights you gained from your experiences.

    How did you start identifying the real needs of your audience or clients?¶

    • Discuss the methods and strategies you used to understand true needs.

    What were some of the common mistakes you made, and how did you correct them?¶

    • Share mistakes and the steps you took to learn from them.

    Success Stories¶

    Can you share an example of a project that succeeded after you shifted your focus to value creation?¶

    • Highlight a specific project that was successful and explain why.

    What made this project different from the ones that didn’t achieve financial success?¶

    • Compare the successful project with less successful ones to show the differences.

    How did focusing on value creation impact the outcomes of your projects?¶

    • Discuss the tangible results of your new approach.

    Practical Advice¶

    What actionable tips would you give to others who are currently focused on sheer output?¶

    • Provide practical advice for shifting from quantity to value.

    How can developers identify the right projects to work on?¶

    • Offer guidance on market research, user feedback, and solving real problems.

    What should developers focus on to create value and achieve financial success?¶

    • Emphasize key areas of focus for value creation.

    Conclusion¶

    What are the main points you want readers to take away from your story?¶

    • Summarize the key lessons and insights.

    How can readers apply these lessons to their own projects and careers?¶

    • Encourage readers to reflect on their work and consider how they can shift their approach.

    What final thoughts do you want to leave with your readers?¶

    • End with an inspiring message about the importance of creating value and making an impact.

    Call to Action¶

    How can readers engage with you or learn more about your work?¶

    • Provide links to your website, social media, or consultation services.

    What steps can readers take to start focusing on value creation today?¶

    • Encourage them to take the first step in shifting their approach and offer ways to get started.
